Muntaha Tasnim

PhD Student, School of Computing, University of Utah

I am a PhD student in the School of Computing at the University of Utah. My research interest lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Security & Privacy. My research explores user interaction with privacy-invasive technologies and how we can design and understand systems that help users manage and protect their privacy more effectively. I am currently working as a Research Assistant with Dr. Sameer Patil. Before starting my PhD, I spent three years working as a Software Engineer in Quality Assurance at Therap (BD) Ltd., Bangladesh.
